# 🎤 Speaker Notes — 5 Minutes
### "From Branch to Brain: Why Your Bank Should Think For You"

---

## [0:00 – 0:45] The Hook — Relatable Pain

> "Raise your hand if you've ever logged into your banking app, stared at your balance, and thought — *okay, so what do I do with this?*"

*Pause. Let it land.*

> "You see a number. Maybe it's good, maybe it's not. But the app says nothing. It doesn't know your rent went up last month. It doesn't know you've been spending £40 a week on coffee. And it certainly doesn't tell you that if you just moved £200 into a Cash ISA right now, you'd earn an extra £180 by Christmas."

> "That silence — that's the gap between traditional banking and what we built."

---

## [0:45 – 1:45] What Traditional Banking Actually Gives You

> "Traditional banking gives you a ledger. A very beautiful, very polished ledger. You can see what came in, what went out, and that's roughly where the conversation ends."

> "If you want advice, you book an appointment. You wait two weeks. You sit across from someone who pulls up a generic brochure and says — 'have you considered a savings account?' Meanwhile you're thinking, *I came here at 2pm on a Tuesday for this?*"

> "And that advisor? They don't know your transaction history the way a system can. They don't know that your biggest expense last month was a subscription you forgot you had. They're working from a snapshot. A 20-minute snapshot."

---

## [1:45 – 3:00] What This Agent Does Differently

> "What we built is fundamentally different. And I want to show you why with a real example."

> "Take Bob. 52 years old, works for the City Council. He asks a simple question — *where should I put my savings?*"

> "In the time it takes you to read this slide, the agent has already done this:"

- ✅ Verified Bob's identity
- ✅ Pulled his full account and balance breakdown
- ✅ Analysed his last transactions — income, top expenses, patterns
- ✅ Calculated his exact monthly surplus — down to the penny
- ✅ Checked every product he's eligible for — not just the standard three
- ✅ Generated a personalised 6-section recommendation with projected year-end returns

> "Bob didn't book an appointment. Bob didn't wait. Bob got a response that said — *your Council Tax is your biggest deductible, your monthly surplus is £2,739, and here's exactly how a Regular Saver at 5% and a Cash ISA at 4.5% would grow your money over the next 12 months.*"

> "That is not a brochure. That is a financial advisor in your pocket."

---

## [3:00 – 4:00] The Day-to-Day Impact

> "Think about how this changes everyday decisions."

> "Monday morning, you get paid. Instead of guessing how much to move into savings — you ask. The agent knows your bills cycle, your average spend, and your buffer. It tells you: *move £500 this week, keep £300 liquid.*"

> "You're thinking about a holiday. You ask — *can I afford it without touching my ISA?* It doesn't just say yes or no. It shows you what dipping into your savings does to your year-end projection."

> "You're 38 and you haven't thought about retirement. It flags the Lifetime ISA — the one with the 25% government bonus — and tells you this is your last window of eligibility. That single nudge could be worth thousands."

> "This is not a chatbot that answers FAQs. This is proactive, contextual, *personalised* intelligence."

---

## [4:00 – 5:00] The Bigger Picture — Close Strong

> "We talk a lot about financial inclusion. About making good financial advice available to everyone — not just the people who can afford a wealth manager."

> "This agent doesn't care if you have £500 or £50,000. It gives Bob the same quality of thinking it gives anyone. Real data. Real products. Real numbers."

> "Traditional banking tells you *what you have.* This agent tells you *what to do with it* — and what it'll be worth if you do."

> "The branch is not going away. But the branch appointment for basic financial guidance? That's already obsolete."

*Pause.*

> "We just built what replaces it."

---

## ⏱ Timing Guide

| Block | Time | Key Beat |
|-------|------|----------|
| Hook | 0:00–0:45 | Audience self-identifies with the problem |
| Traditional banking gap | 0:45–1:45 | Make the status quo feel inadequate |
| Live demo narrative (Bob) | 1:45–3:00 | Concrete, specific, impressive |
| Day-to-day scenarios | 3:00–4:00 | Makes it real for everyone in the room |
| Close | 4:00–5:00 | Memorable, quotable final line |

---

## 💡 Delivery Tips

- **Slow down on the hook.** The rhetorical question only works if the audience has a moment to nod along.
- **The Bob section is your demo anchor.** If you're doing a live demo, start it right after "What we built is fundamentally different."
- **The final two lines are your soundbite.** Deliver them slowly, with a beat between each.
- **Eye contact during the close.** Put the slides down, look at the judges, and land the last line directly.
